About Yertland Montessori School

Yertland Montessori School is a premier educational institution dedicated to providing a nurturing and enriching learning environment for children. Rooted in the Montessori philosophy, our school emphasizes individualized learning, fostering curiosity, creativity, and a lifelong love of learning in each student.

Mission of Yertland Montessori School

Our mission at Yertland Montessori School is to cultivate a supportive and stimulating environment where children can develop academically, socially, and emotionally. We aim to inspire a passion for knowledge and to empower our students to become confident, independent, and compassionate individuals.

Educational Approach

At Yertland Montessori, we follow the Montessori method of education, which is based on self-directed activity, hands-on learning, and collaborative play. Our classrooms are designed to encourage exploration and discovery, allowing students to learn at their own pace under the guidance of experienced and dedicated teachers.

Programs Offered

We offer a range of programs tailored to different age groups, including:

Early Childhood Education: For children aged 2.5 to 6 years, focusing on sensory-based learning and foundational skills.

Elementary Education: For students aged 6 to 12 years, emphasizing critical thinking, problem-solving, and in-depth exploration of subjects.

Facilities

Our state-of-the-art campus is equipped with modern facilities that enhance the learning experience. These include:

  • Spacious, well-equipped classrooms
  • A well-stocked library
  • Science and computer labs
  • Art and music studios
  • A gymnasium and outdoor play areas

Extracurricular Activities

We offer a wide range of extracurricular activities, including sports, arts, music, and various clubs, to ensure the holistic development of our students. Our programs are designed to help children discover their interests and talents, build confidence, and develop teamwork and leadership skills.

Community and Values

Yertland Montessori School is more than just an educational institution; it is a community where students, teachers, and parents collaborate to create a supportive and inclusive atmosphere. We value diversity, respect, and kindness, and we strive to instill these values in our students.
